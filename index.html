About Us

Welcome to the Stay Aligned Foundation!


We are a non-profit organization dedicated to raising awareness about the negative impact of mobile phone use on body posture.

In today's world, we're constantly glued to our screens. While technology offers incredible benefits, it can also take a toll on our physical health. Poor posture, often referred to as "tech neck" when caused by excessive phone use, can lead to a variety of problems, including neck pain, headaches, and spinal misalignment.

At the Stay Aligned Foundation, we believe that good posture is essential for overall well-being. We strive to educate people of all ages about the importance of maintaining a healthy spine while using mobile devices.
